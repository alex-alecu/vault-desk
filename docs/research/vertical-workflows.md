# Vertical Workflows Research

Created: 2026-07-10

Vault Desk should start with professional workflows where document volume, sensitivity, repetition, and ROI are clear.

## Accounting

Accounting is the strongest first vertical.

Reasons:

- High document volume.
- Repetitive tasks.
- Sensitive financial data.
- Clear time savings.
- Measurable return on investment.
- Lower regulatory risk than autonomous medical use.

## Legal

Legal is attractive because the work is document-heavy and citation-sensitive.

Risks:

- High accuracy expectations.
- Formatting and tracked-change requirements.
- Confidentiality obligations.
- Matter-level access controls.

## Medical Administration

Medical administration is a later target.

The product should avoid diagnosis, treatment recommendation, and triage. Safer workflows include organizing records, drafting clinician-approved summaries, extracting form data, and searching documents under strict permissions.

## Other Candidates

Other possible users:

- Financial advisers.
- Insurance offices.
- Executives.
- Consultants.
- Real-estate professionals.
- Small manufacturers.
- Businesses with confidential internal knowledge.

## Workflow Selection Criteria

Choose workflows with:

- Repeated document inputs.
- Clear expected outputs.
- Low need for autonomous external action.
- Human review already present in the business process.
- Strong source-citation value.
- Measurable time savings.
- Manageable regulatory exposure.
